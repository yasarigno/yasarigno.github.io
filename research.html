Research

In my PhD thesis I have studied the theory of Teichmüller spaces of surfaces of topologically infinite type. The first two of the following papers will be soon submitted to Arxiv and also appear here.

  1. Infinite-dimensional Teichmüller spaces
  2. Ideal triangulations of surfaces of infinite type and quasiconformal Teichmüller spaces (joint with Athanase Papadopoulos et Daniele Alessandrini)
  3. A survey on line complexes on the simply connected Riemann surfaces
